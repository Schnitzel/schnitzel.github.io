A bit about me

My name is Michael Schmid, but everybody calls me “Schnitzel”. The nickname began during my school days and stuck with me as my online presence grew. You may know me from DrupalCon as the long-haired guy yelling directions to you from behind the camera for the official group photo. I'm CTO of Amazee Labs Zurich.

small bio

IT Apprenticeship – Siemens Schweiz AG

From beginning on I knew I wanna be an IT guy. So I got my first education with an apprenticeship in IT at Siemens. Even though the team I was working in built hughe networks, I couldn't let go from building websites for the company and in my free time.

2004 - 2008

First lieutenant – Swiss Air Force

As most swiss men, I joined the Swiss Army. As planes and helicopters where my passion, I applied for the selection as a military pilot. As an officer I learned a lot about managing and teamleading.

2008 - 2009

CTO & Member of the Board – Amazee Labs Zurich

I joined Amazee in 2009, at the time, a startup founded around a product, which I would help grow into a new business model. We relaunched as Amazee Labs. At the time there were only 6 employees with myself one of the only two developers. Today I’m the CTO, member of the board and shareholder of Amazee Labs Zurich. We are 20 employees and build Drupal sites for international clients.

2009 - today

Founder – Amazee Holding

The Amazee Holding is an international organization specializing in web development and online marketing. So far we've founded Amazee Metrics, which provides SEO and Analytics; and Amazee Labs Austin, which builds Drupal websites for customers based in Austin, TX.

2013 - today
